# Exact purpose of LAOS v8

LAOS v8 is a control framework used by a highly capable Software Architect AI to understand an application, make the important architecture and risk decisions, and convert that intelligence into small, exact, verifiable instructions for weaker AI agents.

The weaker agents do not receive master LAOS. They receive one authorised action at a time. LAOS constrains what they may do, checks what actually happened, preserves evidence, requires independent review, and reveals the next action only when the current one is proven complete.

For an existing application, the Architect first compiles a read-only investigation workflow. Weaker agents return structured App Intelligence and evidence. The Architect validates it, forms its own understanding, and only then compiles continuation work.

The goal is to make weaker agents perform far more reliably by lending them the Architect’s planning, context, boundaries, verification discipline, and recovery process—without giving them uncontrolled authority.
